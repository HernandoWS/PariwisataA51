Indonesia Tourism Destinations

Bali

The island of Paradise, beautiful beaches, with temples spread allover the island, being the last majority Hindu population in Indonesia, the Island has it's own distinct feeling.

Belitung

The Island where the Rainbow Troops (Laskar Pelangi) came from. With white sand beaches and abstact granite coastlines and other small islands dotting the coastline, the island has a view like no other.

Dieng

A beautiful volcanic complex with a plateau, a multi-colored lake and a high peak which pierces through the clouds. The Mountain is hard to get to but is damn beautiful.

Ujung Kulon

A sanctuary of animals on the Western tip of Java, being the only place with the endangere one horned Javan Rhino, the place is highly protected and conserved. the beauty there is nice change of pace from the sprawling buildings of java.
